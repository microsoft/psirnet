lge_all: csv file for the tyger reconstructions (compatible with the nihrawdata repo)
lge_all_buffers_dim: after tyger reconstructions, obtain the buffer_ids and log them
lge_filtered_large: The following condition applied to `lge_all_buffers_dim.csv`
    condition = (buffers['num_coils'] >= 16) & (buffers['recon_size_y'] >= 144) \
        & (buffers['recon_size_y'] == buffers['enc_size_y']) \
        & (buffers['recon_size_x'] == buffers['enc_size_x'] // 2) \
        & (buffers['num_averages'] >= 8)
lge_filtered_most_common: First, the following condition applied to `lge_filtered_large.csv`
    condition = (buffers['num_coils'] == 30) & (buffers['recon_size_x'] == 256) & \
        (buffers['recon_size_y'] == 192)
    So, num_coils, recon_size_x, recon_size_y, enc_size_x, enc_size_y = (30, 256, 192, 512, 192) for all
    We drop these because they are redundant
    Second, the buffers are processed and the error-free ones are retained.
